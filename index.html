7 Biggest Mistakes in My Creative Journey

Introduction

Ashton Joe's video, "7 Biggest Mistakes in My Creative Journey," offers a reflective look into the trials and errors he faced during his content creation journey on YouTube. Beginning in 2020, Ashton shares the lessons learned from the key mistakes he made, aiming to guide other creators in navigating their own creative paths more smoothly.

The Crucial Mistakes and Lessons Learned

1. Overthinking

One of the first challenges Ashton discusses is overthinking. He details how minor concerns about camera settings, appearance, and angles can deplete creative energy and needlessly complicate the process. His advice is simple: embrace imperfections and focus on gradual improvements.

2. Operating from Scarcity

Operating from a mindset of scarcity, focusing excessively on likes and views for validation, was another significant mistake. Ashton emphasizes the importance of internal work to strengthen creative foundations and shift away from external approval.

3. Rushing into Monetization

Ashton reflects on his premature attempt to monetize his channel, driven by financial need, which only added pressure and distracted from creating meaningful content. He recommends building skills and confidence through client work before seeking broader recognition.

4. Inconsistency

Inconsistency in posting, as Ashton explains, often stems from internal struggles and a lack of self-worth. He suggests maintaining a regular posting schedule to help find one's unique voice and build a loyal audience.

5. Not Playing the YouTube Game

Initially resistant to optimizing his content for YouTube's algorithms, Ashton learned the hard way that compelling titles and thumbnails are crucial for attracting viewers. He advises other creators not to overlook these elements.

6. Lack of Focus on Content Value

Ashton admits to not focusing enough on providing value in his early videos, which resulted in directionless content. He stresses the importance of making each video useful and engaging to ensure it resonates with viewers.

7. Failure to Create Systems

Failing to establish effective production systems was a critical oversight for Ashton, affecting his consistency and content quality. He shares how developing a system that integrates his creative process into his lifestyle has made content creation more enjoyable and sustainable.

Conclusion

Throughout his video, Ashton Joe underscores the importance of authenticity, patience, and viewing the creative journey as a long-term commitment. His experiences and the solutions he offers aim to inspire other creators to navigate their paths with greater awareness and fewer obstacles. Additionally, Ashton promotes his "Dream Flow Operating System," a tool designed to help creators manage their workflow and enhance productivity.
